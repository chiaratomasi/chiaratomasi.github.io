Chiara Tomasi

Full Professor of Economics, University of Trento

About

Chiara Tomasi is Full Professor of Economics at the University of Trento. Her research focuses on industrial dynamics and international trade, with particular attention to firm heterogeneity, productivity, and export behavior. More recently, her work has expanded to environmental and resource economics, including environmental trade policies, critical raw materials, and the transition toward sustainable and circular economic systems.

Research

Her research has been published in journals such as the European Economic Review, Review of Economics and Statistics, Industrial and Corporate Change, Regional Studies, and the Journal of Comparative Economics.
