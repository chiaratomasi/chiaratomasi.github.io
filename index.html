Chiara Tomasi

Full Professor of Economics, University of Trento

About

I am a Full Professor of Economics at the University of Trento. My research focuses on industrial dynamics and international trade, with particular attention to firm heterogeneity, productivity, export behavior, and the role of institutions and regulation in shaping firm performance.

Research

Her research has been published in journals such as the European Economic Review, Review of Economics and Statistics, Industrial and Corporate Change, Regional Studies, and the Journal of Comparative Economics.
